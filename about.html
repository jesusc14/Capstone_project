About Our WebApp

This is a capstone project analyzing transit data and visualizing it can involve gathering and processing data from various sources, such as transit authorities or third-party providers. The data can then be analyzed and interpreted to provide insights into transit patterns, passenger behavior, and other relevant information. Visualization techniques, such as charts, maps, and graphs, can be employed to display the data in a meaningful way and aid in communicating the findings to stakeholders. The project can also involve developing predictive models to anticipate future transit trends and optimize operations. Overall, the goal of the capstone project is to use data analysis and visualization techniques to improve transit systems and enhance the user experience for passengers.
